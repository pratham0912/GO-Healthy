Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about GoHealthy, our features, and how we help you eat better.

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It is a simple calculation that uses your height and weight to estimate whether you are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. The formula is: BMI = weight (kg) / height (m)2. While BMI is a useful screening tool, it does not directly measure body fat or account for muscle mass, age, or gender differences. On GoHealthy, we use BMI alongside other metrics like BMR and TDEE to give you a more complete picture of your health.

Our Smart Meal Planner takes your personal details — age, weight, height, activity level, and fitness goal (weight loss, muscle gain, or maintenance) — and calculates your daily calorie needs using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation. It then determines your optimal macro split (protein, carbs, fats) and generates a full weekly meal plan with breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks tailored to your requirements. You can regenerate or customize your plan at any time.

Nutri AI is our intelligent nutrition assistant powered by advanced AI. When you chat with Nutri AI, it considers your dietary preferences, allergies, calorie targets, and health goals to generate personalized meal suggestions and diet advice. It can answer nutrition questions, suggest recipes, create meal plans for specific conditions (like diabetes or PCOS), and even help you find healthier alternatives to your favorite foods — all through a simple chat interface.
